HOW IT WORKS:
============================================================

HOW LANGUAGE DETECTION WORKS:
─────────────────────────────

1. System checks your message for Hindi words like:
   - mera, kitna, kya, hai, chahiye, sone, kharcha, etc.

2. If Hindi words found → Responds in HINDI/HINGLISH
   
3. If mostly English → Responds in ENGLISH

4. Mix both languages → System picks based on keyword content
